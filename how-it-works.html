Our process

How PawMatch Works

Great matches aren't luck — they're built on honest conversations about your life, and honest profiles of every animal's needs. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you start to the day you bring someone home.

Four steps to your new companion

No cattle calls, no guesswork — just a clear path forward.

1

Tell us about you

Share your home setup, activity level, allergies, work schedule, and experience with animals through a short online questionnaire.

2

We assess & shortlist

Our team cross-references your answers with each animal's temperament notes, health needs, and behavioral history to suggest real fits — not just the cutest photo.

3

Meet & greet

Visit your shortlisted matches in person or at a foster home. Ask questions, spend real time together, and see how everyone feels.

4

Adopt & settle in

Finalize paperwork with our team, get a personalized care plan, and bring your new companion home with ongoing support from us.

What actually goes into a match

We don't just match by breed or cuteness. Every animal in our care is assessed across several dimensions, and we weigh those against your lifestyle to find the pairing most likely to thrive long-term.

  • ✓ Temperament & energy level — calm companion vs. high-energy adventurer, social vs. independent.
  • ✓ Health & medical needs — chronic conditions, mobility, dietary requirements, and vet history.
  • ✓ Household compatibility — kids, other pets, apartment vs. yard, noise sensitivity.
  • ✓ Time & experience — training needs, exercise requirements, and how much hands-on care fits your schedule.

A quick example

A first-time renter in a small apartment who works long hours might be a wonderful fit for Biscuit, a low-energy senior cat who needs a quiet routine and a soft lap — but not the right match for Scout, a young herding mix who needs acreage and daily runs.

Both deserve great homes. Our job is finding the one that fits.

What to expect, timeline-wise

Every journey is different, but here's a typical pace.

Day 1

Submit your questionnaire — takes about 10 minutes.

Day 2–4

Our team reviews your answers and sends a shortlist of 2–4 potential matches with full profiles.

Week 1–2

Schedule and attend meet & greets at your own pace — no pressure to decide on the spot.

Adoption day

Sign paperwork, receive a personalized care guide, and take your new companion home.

First 30 days

We check in with you and offer support for training, health questions, or settling-in challenges.
