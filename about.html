Our Story

Matching isn't luck. It's the whole point.

PawMatch started with a simple frustration: too many adoptions were guesswork, and too many animals ended up back at the shelter through no fault of their own. We built a better way — one that looks at real compatibility, not just a cute photo.

Compatibility first, always.

Every animal has a story, and so does every home. Our mission is to bring those stories together thoughtfully — matching lifestyle, health needs, energy level, and temperament so that adoption day is the beginning of a lasting relationship, not a trial run.

Instead of browsing endless listings and hoping for the best, PawMatch asks the questions that matter: Do you have young kids or other pets? Are you looking for a jogging partner or a quiet companion? Can you manage a chronic health condition or a shy, slow-to-trust personality? We use those answers to surface animals who are genuinely a fit — for your life as it actually is, not as a shelter profile assumes it to be.

What we match on

  • Household setup — kids, other pets, apartment or yard
  • Activity level and daily routine
  • Health needs and required veterinary care
  • Temperament — sociable, independent, or needing patience
  • Experience level with training and behavioral support

One nationwide network, one shared goal

PawMatch partners with more than 80 shelters and rescues across the country, from busy urban intake centers to small volunteer-run rescues. We work directly with their staff and behavior teams to keep every animal profile accurate, current, and honest — so the match you make is based on real information, not guesswork.

That network means more animals get seen, more shelters get support with placement, and more families find the companion who actually fits their life.
