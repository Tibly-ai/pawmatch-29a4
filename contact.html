Quick Answers

How soon can I meet an animal?

Most meet-and-greets can be scheduled within 2–3 days of your request, depending on the animal's availability and any current holds.

Do I need an appointment to visit?

Walk-ins are welcome during open hours, but scheduling ahead ensures a counselor is free to walk you through introductions.

Can you help me choose the right animal?

Absolutely — tell us about your home, experience, and lifestyle in the form, and a counselor will suggest good-fit matches based on temperament and care needs.
